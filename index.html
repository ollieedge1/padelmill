What is padel?
Padel is a racquet sport that combines elements of tennis and squash. It's played in doubles on an enclosed court about a third of the size of a tennis court. The walls are in play, making rallies longer and more fun. It's easy to learn and incredibly social.
When will Padel Mill open?
We're targeting 2027 for opening. We're currently securing a site near Cambridge city centre and working through the planning process. Join the waitlist to get updates and founding member pricing.
Do you offer training?
Yes — we'll offer group sessions, private coaching, junior programmes, and a structured academy for all levels from complete beginners to competitive players.
Can events be hosted at Padel Mill?
Absolutely. We'll offer corporate team building, private event hire, birthday parties, and company leagues. Whether you're 4 or 100 people, we can help organise your event.
Where will you be located?
We're securing a warehouse space within 2 miles of Cambridge city centre, close to the station and the science parks. Exact location will be confirmed soon — join the waitlist for updates.
